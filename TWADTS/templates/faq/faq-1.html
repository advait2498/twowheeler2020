FAQ

1. What is VADS?

VADS stands for Vehicle Accident Detection System. We ensure people have a safe ride and we help tracking and monitoring the vehicle of your dear ones.

2. What services do VADS provide? VADS provides services like :-

Live Tracking

Message Alert

E-mail Alert

Crash Detection

Parental Logins

Insurance Claims

Hospital Claims

Roadside Assistance etc

3. Is VADS free?

No. VADS is not a free service. Once you are done with the trial, you have to pay for further services.

4. Can I choose the services provided in custom manner?

Yes. You can choose the services as per your wish.

5. Is VADS safe?

Yes. VADS is safe by all means. Your data is safe with us.

6. What hardware do we get from VADS?

You will be provided with a hardware kit according to the services you choose.

7. Is VADS compatible for both Android and iOS? Currently we are only working on website.

You will be provided with a hardware kit according to the services you choose.

8. How do I contact to VADS for any queries?

You can contact us by writing your queries on the feedback page.

9. Do I need to put my location ON 24/7?

No. You need to switch ON your location only when using the services like tracking, monitoring, Parental Control, etc.

10. Is VADS compatible with all vehicles?

No. VADS is compatible only with two-wheelers.

11. Do you provide free trial service?

Yes. We do provide a free trial with limited services and for limited amount of time.

12. How do I become a premium member of VADS?

You can do so by selecting the premium package while becoming a member.

13. How long do I have access to my data?

As long as you are an active customer you have access to your data.

14. How can I track my vehicle from my mobile phone?

By clicking the “Track” button and by switching the hardware and location ON.

15. What special services do I get if I am a premium member of VADS?

Roadside Assistance, Insurance claims, 24/7 support are some of the services which premium members will get.
